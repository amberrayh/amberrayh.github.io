Hi there! My name is Amber and I'm excited to be starting a new career as a UX Researcher and Designer.

I've always been interested in storytelling. I've been writing poetry since I was five. I have an artistic bent too and enjoy sketching, crafting and learning how to use new design software.

After graduating from college with a degree in English I've spent the last 8 years in the business world and have loved learning how to manage a team, balance the books, and (most of all) how to look at what's costing a company money and what's making a company money and to maximize the money making!

I love the lean startup mindset. I'm thrifty when it comes to budgeting and when it comes to design!

In my free time I read voraciously, cook, and animal watch. New hobbies include snorkeling and scuba diving, golf, and weight lifting.

I'm excited to get started with new projects and to help more businesses grow through UX practices, so please say hello.

I look forward to speaking with you!

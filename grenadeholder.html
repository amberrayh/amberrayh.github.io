GRENADE HOLDER




Designing an iOS gaming app is fun! Especially when kids are involved. This app was an idea that my step-son came up with and he did the paper and pencil sketches of it on his own. We worked on wireframes in Sketch together and he's been helping his Dad learn to program it with Swift.





CHALLENGE

  • Designing for a viral gaming app invented by an 11 year old.
  • Stay true to the creator's vision and preferences for color, special effects, etc.
  • Provide feedback throughout the development process to ensure a quality user experience.

WIREFRAMES



My 11-year-old step son and I worked on these wireframes together (I was driving) based on the sketches that he had already done. We focused on having bright colors, cool graphics, and large, obvious navigation.

FINAL MOCKUPS & DEVELOPMENT




This app is currently in development. Look for it in the itunes app store soon!

TREASURE CHEST




For this project I was asked to take mom & pop's successful brick and mortar toy store online in the form of an e-commerce website.

NAVIGATION


Mom and Pop's store focuses on high-quality classic and retro toys and magic tricks and so those types of toys were highlighted via categories. Breadcrumbs let users know where they are. Global navigation in the header and footer provide direct access to a shopping cart and information about the brick and mortar store.

USER FLOWS


Design was done with 3 specific user flows in mind. One for finding a product via search, one for finding an age appropriate magic trick (shown above), and one for completing checkout once a toy had been selected.

UI DESIGN


For the checkout process my design included a one-click checkout for returning guests as well as an option for guest checkout.

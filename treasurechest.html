TREASURE CHEST




Mom and Pop's successful brick and mortar toy store is making its online debut with an e-commerce website.





CHALLENGE

  • Oganize Mom & Pop's toy inventory so that it is easy to navigate and reflects their careful selection of classic toys and magic tricks.
  • Ceate beautiful product pages.
  • Design an intuitive checkout process that takes into account the needs of many different users.

CONTENT STRATEGY



Categorizing Mom & Pop's toy inventory was a process of trial and error as well as looking at how the big players like Amazon and Google Shopping categorize their toys. In the end I came up with 5 main categories that could easily be displayed across the top of the website. These categories reflect what is important to the Treasure Chest toy store: classic and retro toys as well as magic and novelty items.

USER FLOWS & SITE MAPS




The next step in the design process was to create user flows and a site map in order to plan how many screens would need to be created and how they would be connected.

UI DESIGN



My final design included consistent breadcrumbs and category tabs throughout so that users always know where they are, product pages that make the toy the star, and an intuitive checkout process with one-click checkout for returning customers.

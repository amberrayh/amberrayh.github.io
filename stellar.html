STELLAR




Everfave is a social couponing app where users can earn rewards for their favorite stores and brands when they share them through social media. Our client asked us to conduct interviews and user testing in order to answer a few questions regarding this app:

1. Are users willing to complete the process of sharing stores and brands on their social media in order to get a coupon?

2. Does icon placement of the different sharing options affect which option users will choose?

3. Would users customize the sharing message with a picture if they could?

4. If the user were the one receiving the shared message from a friend, would it be more appealing if their friend had shared a picture?

5. Do they prefer to sign up before they see the coupon or after (but before they can redeem)?

USABILITY TESTING




SHARING FLOW




Our usability testing found that users were willing to complete the sharing process in order to receive a coupon. Users generally had a specific method of sharing (text, facebook, etc.) that they were comfortable with and moving the icons for these methods around did not dissuade them from finding the one that they were comfortable with. Users prefered to receive a customized message with a picture from their friend, but did not want to go through the effort of customizing the message with a picture themselves. Users prefered to be able to see a blurred out coupon with a sign-in button over it, rather than a sign-in page only (as seen above).


RECOMMENDATIONS


In further testing we would be interested in making message customization (with a picture) mandatory and seeing if the users would still be willing to complete the process. We also made some recommendations to our client regarding the home ("Discover") screen of the app (as seen above).

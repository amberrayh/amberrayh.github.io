MY MARATHON COACH




Designing an app to solve a classmate’s problem...

My classmate Malcolm had a problem finding a marathon training program that took his special circumstances into consideration. Malcolm had never run a marathon before, but was not a complete novice to running either. His marathon was a long way out, but he wanted to stay on track with his exercise.

Malcolm also needed his schedule to be flexible enough that he could change things without getting totally off track. And he wanted to be able to track his progress.

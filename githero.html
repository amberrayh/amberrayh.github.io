GIT HERO




Git Hero is a web service that links business owners with developers and designers in order to propel their digital products forward. Our model is meant to take out middle managers allowing business owners to get the work they need done for cheap.



CHALLENGE

  • Design for 2 user sets: business/project owners and the people who will work on the projects.
  • Solidify a user flow for project owners.
  • Design a compelling splash page in order to generate interest in the website/service and test market value.

USER NEEDS ANALYSIS



Designing for interactions between multiple user sets can be tricky and is often a stumbling block for initial product design. It was important that we understood our 2 types of users needs well before proceeding with our design. Ultimately we decided to focus on the needs of our business owners first and design for them in our MVP.

STORYBOARDING & USER FLOWS




Storyboarding helped to solidify understanding of our business owner's needs and we were then able to begin planning a user flow and the necessary features to make that flow successful.

SPLASH PAGE DESIGN



Once we had a good understanding of what the user flow for our business owner would entail we decided to take a step back and create a compelling splash page where business owners could begin to submit their ideas. We wanted our splash page to make it clear what differentiates Git Hero from a standard design agency or competitor site such as Gigster. The easy-to-use idea submission form will allow us to begin to guage what type of interest exists for this product.
